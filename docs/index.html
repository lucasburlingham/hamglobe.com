HamGlobe

Put your logs on the map

See Your QSOs Come Alive

Imagine uploading a log file and instantly watching your contacts trace lines across the planet. HamGlobe turns any point-to-point log (Amateur Radio or otherwise) into an interactive map that you can share, embed, and export.

Powerful Features, Simple Workflow

  • Instantly visualize log entries as arcs and points on a globe
  • Embed a live, interactive map directly on your personal site or blog
  • Download high-resolution images for newsletters, slides, or print
  • ADIF support for seamless import from your logging software
  • QRZ lookup brings station details into the map automatically
  • Choose from multiple base layers and color schemes
  • Filter by date, band, mode, and other criteria for custom views

Installation

HamGlobe is available as a web application and can be accessed at https://hamglobe.com. No installation is required; simply create an account and start uploading your logs.

Usage

Upload your log file in ADIF format, and HamGlobe will automatically parse the data and display it on the map. You can customize the map style, filter logs by various criteria, and share through a unique URL or API.
